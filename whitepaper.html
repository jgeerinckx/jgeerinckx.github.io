The Pressure: Energy, Difficulty & Cost Scaling

⛮ Network Difficulty (Global Pressure)

Difficulty is a single global number that rises with total active hashpower. It is the primary pressure valve that prevents early players from permanent dominance.

  • Purpose: Gradually increases Energy consumption across the system.
  • Effect: Reduces the profitability of older hardware, forcing reinvestment.
  • Safety: The Difficulty is hard-capped at 10 forever to prevent runaway costs and crashes.

⚡ Dynamic Cost Scaling

Energy costs scale in three dynamic ways, mirroring real-world hardware depreciation:

  • By Miner Generation: Newer miners consume more but are power-efficient.
  • By Network Difficulty: Higher difficulty increases energy costs across the entire system.
  • By Miner Age: Older miners become disproportionately less energy-efficient relative to new tech. This naturally phases out obsolete hardware.

The Hardware: Generations & Recycling

Generational Tech (NFTs)

Miners are NFTs progressing through 20 generations. Each new generation represents a major leap:

  • » Significantly More Powerful ($1.67 \times$ Hashpower).
  • » More Resistant to future Difficulty increases.
  • » Forces a permanent reinvestment loop to stay competitive.

The Burn Reward (MBR)

To prevent inventory clutter, players can permanently destroy (burn) obsolete miners.

  • » Returns a fraction of the original mint price in CREDIT.
  • » Return value decays exponentially based on how many newer generations exist.
  • » Acts as a guaranteed exit value and economy cleanup tool.

Player-Funded Research

New generations are unlocked entirely by the community's investment in research using CREDIT.

  • » Investment ROI: Funders receive 25% of all lifetime sales of that new generation, proportionally.
  • » Creates speculative incentives for early backers and absorbs CREDIT during growth.

Strategic Layer: Solo vs Guild & Facilities

The Choice: Risk vs Stability

Choose between maximum raw profit or strategic cooperation.

Solo Mining (100% ROI)

Higher volatility, maximum independence, no access to facilities. High Risk, High Raw Reward.

Guild Mining (3% Tax)

Lower volatility, pooled hashrate, access to powerful Facilities. Stability, Strategic Advantage.

Anti-Whale Rule: No single Guild can contribute more than 5% of total network hashpower.

Strategic Guild Facilities

Facilities are temporary, exclusive infrastructure bonuses (10-14 days) built using CREDIT. They reduce risk and improve efficiency, but never grant brute-force profitability.

  • Cooling Station: Reduces daily energy cost for all members.
  • Predictive Hub: Provides early warning before major Difficulty updates (risk control).
  • Buffer Extender: Extends maximum energy hours to prevent downtime during inactivity.
  • Research Accel.: Increases members' effective research funding impact (sourced from Treasury).
  • Emergency Reserve: One-time automatic energy refill protection against sudden outages.

Long-Term Sustainability

The Treasury: Decades of Operation

The Treasury is the non-profit economic backbone, funded by gameplay (guild tax, miner sales, fees). It ensures the game runs for decades without emergency inflation.

  • Funds essential infrastructure.
  • Maintains long-term economic stability.
  • Funds the permanent Tail Emission.
